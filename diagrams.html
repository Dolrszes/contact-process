Diagrams and Graphs

Process flow · Equipment overview · Temperature vs SO₃ yield

A labelled process diagram of the Contact Process illustrates the main stages of sulfuric acid production: sulfur combustion (SO₂ formation), catalytic oxidation (SO₂ → SO₃) using a vanadium(V) oxide (V₂O₅) catalyst, absorption of SO₃ in concentrated H₂SO₄ (oleum formation), and dilution to produce sulfuric acid.

The diagram includes arrows indicating gas and liquid flow directions, and each unit operation is labelled using appropriate scientific terminology.

Figure 2. Effect of temperature on the equilibrium yield of SO₃. As temperature increases, the reaction rate rises but the equilibrium yield of SO₃ decreases, illustrating the exothermic nature of the reaction.
